Return Policy Overview

Understanding our simple and fair return process

30-Day Window

Return any item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. No questions asked for most agricultural products.

Original Condition

Items must be in original packaging and condition. Seeds and perishables have specific return conditions.

Free Shipping

We provide prepaid return labels for all eligible returns. No shipping costs for you.

Quick Refunds

Refunds processed within 5-7 business days once we receive your returned item.

Support Available

Our customer service team is here to help with any return questions or issues.

Quality Guarantee

If you're not satisfied with the quality, we'll make it right with a full refund or exchange.

How to Return Items

Follow these simple steps for a hassle-free return

1
Initiate Return Request

Fill out our return form below or contact our customer service team. You'll need your order number and reason for return.

2
Receive Return Authorization

We'll email you a Return Authorization (RA) number and prepaid shipping label within 24 hours.

3
Pack Your Items

Securely package items in original packaging. Include the RA number and any accessories that came with the product.

4
Ship Your Return

Attach the prepaid label and drop off at any authorized shipping location. Keep your tracking receipt.

5
Get Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.
